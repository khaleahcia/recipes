Ingredients:
- 1 package chicken
- 1 onion
- 1 bell pepper
- cajun seasoning
- water


Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Clean chicken and place in glass baking dish.

2. Slice onion and bell pepper. Add to dish over and around chicken. Season with salt and cajun seasoning.

3. Add water to dish until almost covering chicken. Place aluminum foil over dish.

4. Place dish in oven and let bake 1 1/2 hour. Do not allow all water to evaporate. Make sure chicken is cooked through before serving.
